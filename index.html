How to get your Instagram data

1
Open Instagram → Settings → Your activity → Download your information (or visit instagram.com/download/request)
2
Select JSON format, choose Followers and Following, then request the download. Instagram emails you a link within minutes to a few hours.
3
Download and unzip the file. Inside the connections/followers_and_following/ folder, find followers_1.json and following.json.
4
Upload both files below and hit Analyze. Everything runs in your browser — nothing is uploaded to any server.
👥 followers_1.json Your followers list
➡️ following.json Who you follow
